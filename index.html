MEET KARIN

Karin is a Vancouver based Interior Designer at the helm of her own full-service design agency, House of Bohn. After ten years as an industry leading designer and entrepreneur, Karin has quickly become one of Vancouver’s aspiring personalities through her YouTube channel featuring the ever popular ‘Day In The Life Of A Busy Entrepreneur’ videos, as well as her new web-series ‘#Bohnafide’ which documents her own personal home renovation with the help of her team at House of Bohn.
